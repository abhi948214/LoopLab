How to test with 5 people (learning)
  • Upload this file to Hostinger public_html (e.g. product-demo.html).
  • Set your GA4 ID in two places in the <head> if you use Analytics.
  • Send the public URL to five people; each opens it once on phone or Wi‑Fi.
  • They should see City / Region / Country on this page right away.
  • You open GA4 Realtime during their visit to confirm the tag fires.
  • For a class report: note that IP location is approximate; five visits are enough to prove the idea, not to choose a warehouse.
